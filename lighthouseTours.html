OUR FAVORITE DESTINATIONS
Image Lighthouse Location Things To Do
Bodie Island Outer Banks, NC Extensive restoration project recently completed by National Park Service
Located near Cape Hatteras Lighthouse
Both lighthouses are open for climbing
The 156' ft. lighthouse still shines a beam 19 miles offshore, making full rotations at 27.5 seconds
Split Rock Two Harbors, MN A National Historic Landmark
Climb the lighthouse, take a hike, hear the foghorn
Surrounded by Split Rock Lighthouse State Park with picnic sites, camping, and hiking
Nov 10 Annual Edmund Fitzgerald Commemorative Beacon Lighting
Thomas Point Shoal Thomas Point Shoal, MD Located 2 miles offshore, accessed by boat tours from Annapolis
Built in 1875, last screwpile lighthouse on the Bay still in its original location
For you history buffs, visit the nearby Annapaolis Maratime Museum for fascinating lectures and tours
