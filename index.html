Lighthouse Society passport to fun!


The United States Lighthouse Society Passport Program was designed to generate donations for participating lighthouses and is a fun way to chronicle your lighthouse adventures. As you visit participating lighthouses, you will get a stamp to put in your passport. Click on the passport to visit the Lighthouse Society Passport Program link. As a complimentary gift to our traveling customers, we will provide a lighthouse passport to each member of your traveling group.
